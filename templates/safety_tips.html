Safety Tips and Guidelines

Safety Tips

  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible.
  • Share your location with trusted contacts.
  • In case of emergency, dial the emergency number immediately.
  • Report any suspicious activity to local authorities.
  • Trust your instincts and avoid risky situations.

Emergency Guidelines

  • Stay calm and try to contact emergency services.
  • If you are in immediate danger, find a safe place.
  • Provide clear and accurate information to emergency responders.
  • Follow instructions from emergency services.
  • Keep a list of emergency contacts handy.
