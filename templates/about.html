About Us

Welcome to Abhaya, your go-to platform for personal safety and security. Our mission is to provide users with a comprehensive suite of tools and resources to help them stay safe and informed in their daily lives.

Features

  • User Authentication: Secure login and registration system with session management.
  • Aadhaar Card Verification: Upload and verify Aadhaar card details for registration and authentication.
  • Real-Time Location Tracking: Live updates of user locations for accurate location-based features.
  • Map Integration: Interactive maps with real-time location updates, recent incidents, and safe areas.
  • Analytical Dashboard: Displays user analytics, including gender distribution and recent incidents.
  • Emergency Alerts: A button for sending emergency alerts quickly.
  • Safe Areas Identification: Map view of safe zones for users in critical situations.
  • Complaints Submission: Allows users to submit and view complaints.
  • Recent Incidents Reporting: Detailed reports on recent incidents through embedded iframes.
  • Safety Tips: Provides safety tips and emergency guidelines.
  • Profile Management: Manage and update user profiles, including profile pictures.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark modes for better visibility in different lighting conditions.
